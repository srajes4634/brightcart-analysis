
BrightCart Profitability Summary

1. Category Insights
- Most profitable category: Grocery
- Least profitable category: Sports

2. Channel Insights
- Channels vary significantly in return rates and profit contribution.
- Higher return rates negatively impact profit margins.

3. Marketing Insights
- Best ROAS platform: TikTok Ads
- Worst ROAS platform: Affiliate

4. Recommendation
- Reduce spend on Affiliate and low-margin categories.
- Reallocate budget toward TikTok Ads.
- Focus on channels with lower return rates to improve profitability.
